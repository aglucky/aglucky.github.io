Shoulder Pain Treatment


  • Cross arms so they form a 90 degree angle and use the outside arms to push the inside arm towards the body
  • Look at neck stretch instructions
  • Keep your arms up to the side of your body and rotate them in small circles
  • Stand with your feet wide apart, stretch your arms backward, and lock your palms behind your back
  • Open your chest and bend forward at the hips, simultaneously lifting your locked hands over your head
  • Bend as much as you can and hold this pose for a few seconds before you get back up
  • Put one arm behind your back and use the other arm to pull it up from above, hold for 5-10 seconds
  • Rest if sore
