Forearm Pain Treatment



  • Hold palm out in front of you and pull fingers back until you feel a minor stretch, then hold there for 15-20 seconds for 3 sets alternating arms
  • Hold light weights in your arms with your shoulders relaxed
  • Open and close fingers until you feel a light burn in the forearms
  • Rotate wrists
  • Take a break from typing or other engaging activity
